    Real Results
    Client Case Studies
    Here is what we have helped businesses like yours achieve.
    Beverage Sector
    GSE Listing Achieved
    The Challenge
    A beverage company sought to list on the Ghana Stock Exchange but lacked the environmental and sustainability governance structures required by GSE listing requirements.
    What We Did
    Cleanearth SustainLab designed and implemented an ESG governance framework aligned with GSE requirements, developed sustainability policies, and prepared all required disclosures and documentation.
    Successfully listed on the Ghana Stock Exchange.
    Paint Manufacturing
    Funding Unlocked
    The Challenge
    A paint manufacturing company was unable to access financing due to the absence of a formal Environmental and Social Management System, which lenders required as a condition for funding.
    What We Did
    We designed and implemented a full ESMS aligned with IFC Performance Standards, including environmental risk assessments, management plans, monitoring frameworks, and lender-ready documentation.
    ESMS approved by lender. Funding successfully secured.
    Financial Services
    90-Day ESG Strategy
    The Challenge
    A financial sector institution needed a structured ESG strategy to respond to increasing regulatory expectations, investor requirements, and internal governance demands — within a tight timeframe.
    What We Did
    Cleanearth delivered a comprehensive ESG capacity building programme for the institution's team and developed a bespoke 90-day ESG strategy covering governance, risk, reporting, and stakeholder engagement.
    Full 90-day ESG strategy delivered and adopted.
